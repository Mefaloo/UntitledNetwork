Democracy
At UntitledNetwork strive to give everybody the best experience. In order to achieve this we have an indirect democracy in place as mentioned earlier. In this page we are going to take a deeper look at the inner workings of this system.

The reason we have an indirect democracy rather than a direct one is because we feel having a poll on every choice we make behind the scenes does not only make the development process of our network extremely slow due to the 48 hour vote period, it also bothers the voters with frequent pings. We feel having an elected representative would be the best solution to both of these problems.

The elected community representative is called "The President". The president is elected by the community every two weeks at Sunday 1PM EST, currently this is done through discord reactions. The admin hosting the election will be in charge of adding names to the President elects. If a member is not on the list and seems as if they would do a good job, another member is expected to tag the person they would like to see elected President a discord channel named #PresidentElect along with a tag to the admin hosting the election. Two days after the election got started, the voting is put to a stop and the new president gets assigned their new role and the previous president gets their role removed. (Maybe replace with an Ex-President role in the future)

The President can serve as many terms as he is elected in. Unless they break the rules during their term. Breaking rules as President will cost you the ability to be re-elected. After a recent ban you can not be elected President for 4 times the length of the ban. Meaning that if you were banned for 1 week you can not be elected president for 4 weeks.

If decisions are considered eligible by the President and an Admin, they can be decided upon in a public poll. These polls work similar to the elections and work with a majority. We try not to stop any choices which were voted for by the community, however in some cases there might be need for intervention by staff. Examples of choices that will be stopped are:
1. Choices that give the president more power or a higher chance at getting reelected.
2. Choices that otherwise interfere with the democracy.
3. Choices that are financially or technically unreasonable or unrealistic.

The President can be impeached if a poll vote by an admin makes it obvious people do not approve of the president's behavior, the President gets demoted. After the impeaching of the President we temporarily go without President until the poll for reelection is two days old.

Server resets are the most controversial votes that exist, thus a server reset needs approval of the President, the entire admin team-1 and the poll regarding the reset needs to have at least 75% of votes in favor of a reset.

Rules

These rules override the rules in the /rules command. If you have any questions or concerns about these rules please contact us via discord. If you follow these rules perfectly you will not be punished. In case you do get punished and you know for what but you werenâ€™t violating rules, create a punishment appeal explaining how your actions did not violate the rules.
If you are punished for breaking rules, you are still allowed to appeal.

This rule does not apply to discrimination based on anything directly related to the game. For example not allowing someone into your base because they have the color green in their skin is allowed. What is not allowed is saying things such as "Transgender people are not allowed in my base". Using slurs is also strictly forbidden.

Any form of discrimination will be met with a ban.

Making threats to harm yourself or another person is not allowed. Making threats to break rules could is also not allowed. If the threats are actually followed up (for example you actually release someone's personal information without their permission) you could get permanently banned.

Having an inappropriate name or nickname will get you punished depending on the name or nickname. For example having the name "Adolf Hitler" will get you a ban for discrimination. If you join the server with an inappropriate name your nickname will be set for you and we will recommend you change your name as soon as possible. Setting your nickname to something inappropriate is worse because you are supposed to know the rules well enough by the time you get permission to change your nickname.

Encouraging rule breaking is not allowed. No matter where this rule breaking takes place. This is not limited to UntitledNetwork related platforms. "Hey lets go and advertise UntitledNetwork on play.example.xyz" is not allowed. We love that you want to expand our network but going to bother people by breaking their rules is not the way.

Using our network to promote a brand, network or other servers is prohibited. Discord servers related to UntitledNetwork are an exception, more about this can be found under the discord creation guidelines. This rule also applies to direct messages.

The sharing of not safe for work content, for example sending pictures that were made with sexual intentions in mind, is strictly prohibited. This includes but is not limited to: Gore, pornography and messages with erotic intent.
